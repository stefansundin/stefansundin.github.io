FAQ

How do I get the Discord invite?

After donating a total of $25 or more, email stefaNStefansundinCom with your donation method and the email address used. Once I have verified the donation, I will send your invite.

If you donated using GitHub Sponsors then you should automatically receive the invite.

Does it need to be $25 in one payment?

No. It can be one donation or multiple donations over several months that add up to at least $25.

Is Discord access permanent?

Yes. Once you've gotten access, you do not need to keep donating to remain in the server.

Why Discord and not something else?

Discord currently seems like the best trade-off: it has a low barrier to entry, works well for community conversations, and, well, it is what the cool kids use these days. If something better comes along, we can migrate. For now, Discord seems like the best option.

I donated in the past. Can I still get access?

Yes. Email stefaNStefansundinCom with the donation method, email address used, and approximate dates. If your total support is at least $25, I will send your Discord invite.

What is the best way to get help with a bug or feature request?

Please open a public issue in the relevant GitHub repository. Public issues let other users contribute ideas, confirm bugs, and submit fixes. Once you have opened an issue, you can post the link in Discord and I will try to look at it sooner.

Does donating guarantee that my feature request will be included?

No. Donations support the project as a whole, but they do not guarantee that a particular feature will be added to an official release. I still need to consider whether a change is useful, maintainable, and appropriate for everyone who uses the software.

Can you build or maintain a custom version for me?

Possibly. For sufficiently sponsored work, I can create a long-lived custom branch and provide ready-to-use binaries, even when the change is not suitable for integration to the project. The required sponsorship depends on the scope and ongoing maintenance involved, so these arrangements can be discussed case by case. Start by opening a GitHub issue, or email me if the request needs to remain private.

Can I email you for support questions?

Please use GitHub Issues or Discord for general support questions so answers can help other users too. Email is best reserved for donation verification, private custom-work inquiries, and other matters that should not be public. The $50 monthly GitHub Sponsors tier includes direct email support.

I may not be able to reply to unsolicited support emails.

I do not have a GitHub account. What should I do?

If you do not have a GitHub account and do not want to create one, you are welcome to post your issue directly in Discord instead.
